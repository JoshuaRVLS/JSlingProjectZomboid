title=JSling
description=A Build 42 rifle sling system focused on multiplayer support, flexible carry poses, and better weapon-carry gameplay.
description=
description=What makes this version stand out:
description=- Built with multiplayer support in mind.
description=- Wear up to 2 slings in the base mod.
description=- Optional addon expands that to 4 slings.
description=- Multiple carry poses, including right-waist and left-waist variants.
description=- Extra wearable utility gear like chest rigs, webbing, tactical vest, back rig, knife sheaths, and katana sheath.
description=
description=Core sling features:
description=- Each sling can hold 1 weapon.
description=- Adjustable poses: Front Left, Front Right, Front Center, Back, Right Waist, Left Waist.
description=- One clean visible item name for players, with pose switching handled through context menus.
description=- Automatic sling slot assignment when wearing multiple slings.
description=- Right-click weapon attach/remove support for compatible sling weapons.
description=- Sling-side attach and detach options.
description=- Sling Quick Swap hotkey support.
description=- Timed wear / pose-change progress actions.
description=
description=Multiplayer:
description=- Reworked client/server flow for sling wear, pose changes, attach, and detach actions.
description=- Better validation and sync behavior.
description=- Improved remote visual refresh to reduce desync-prone sling state.
description=- Multiplayer is largely supported, but as with any complex gear/attachment mod, there may still be edge-case bugs that need further polish.
description=
description=World integration:
description=- Container loot spawns for police, military, gun, hunting, and survivalist-style locations.
description=- Police and military zombie outfit support.
description=- Military zombies can also spawn with a slung firearm.
description=
description=Extra wearable gear:
description=- Tactical Vest
description=- Chest Rig
description=- Webbing variants
description=- Back Rig
description=- Knife Sheath variants
description=- Katana Sheath
description=- Extra hotbar attachment slots for rig, vest, sheath, and back carry setups.
description=
description=Optional addons:
description=- Two Hand Melee Addon: allows supported two-handed melee weapons on sling slots.
description=- Four Sling Addon: expands the total simultaneous sling count from 2 to 4.
description=
description=Notes:
description=- Uses the same sling model basis as Noir Rifle Slings, with reworked gameplay, multiplayer handling, and usability improvements.
description=- Best suited for players who want visible weapon carry, more sling pose options, and more wearable gear utility in Build 42.
